Principal Investigator

Dr. Raquel Quatrini

Fundacion Ciencia & Vida — Laboratory of Microbial Ecophysiology

Dr. Quatrini leads the Laboratory of Microbial Ecophysiology, focusing on the ecology, evolution, and molecular physiology of acidophilic microorganisms. Her work integrates genomics, transcriptomics, and field ecology to understand how microbial communities adapt and thrive in extreme environmental conditions.

The lab's current research centers on Acidithiobacillia — bacteria that inhabit highly acidic, metal-rich environments such as volcanic fields and acid mine drainages. Through combining experimental approaches with computational tools, the group aims to decipher the rules governing microbial adaptation at the molecular and community level.

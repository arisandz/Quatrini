Raquel Quatrini

Microbial ecophysiology, acidophile genomics & evolution

Raquel leads the Laboratory of Microbial Ecophysiology at the Fundacion Ciencia & Vida. Her research focuses on understanding how acidophilic microorganisms adapt to extreme geochemical gradients — from the molecular mechanisms of metal resistance to the evolutionary ecology of chemolithotrophic communities. Her group integrates comparative genomics, transcriptomics, and field-based ecology to build a systems-level understanding of life in acid environments.

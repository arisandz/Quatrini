Featured Production
Documentary Film

Title of the Documentary

This fascinating documentary follows a Chilean–Argentine team of scientists in their search for extremophiles—organisms that inhabit some of the most inhospitable environments on Earth—in order to understand how, after billions of years of evolution and environmental change, they continue to persist and thrive. The expedition not only traverses remote and striking landscapes, but also invites reflection on the major challenges facing humanity, from climate change to the search for life on other planets.

// Our Commitment

"Science that stays inside the lab is science half done."

We believe that communicating our research is as fundamental as conducting it. From the acidic rivers of northern Chile to classrooms and screens across the country, we work to make extreme microbiology visible, accessible, and inspiring.

Outreach

Science beyond the lab walls

Connecting Science with Society

We believe science is most powerful when shared. Our outreach efforts aim to bring microbiology, evolution, and extreme environments to broader audiences — from classrooms to the general public.

Science Education

We visit local schools and universities to give talks and hands-on workshops about extremophiles, microbial life, and the scientific method. Making cutting-edge research accessible to young curious minds.

Public Talks

From science festivals to community events, we participate in public forums to discuss extreme environments, astrobiology, and the origin of life. Science should spark curiosity in everyone.

Science Communication

Through articles, social media, and multimedia content, we translate our research into accessible stories — making the invisible world of microorganisms visible and exciting.

Student Mentoring

We actively mentor undergraduate and early-career researchers, offering guidance on lab techniques, scientific writing, and career paths in academia and beyond.

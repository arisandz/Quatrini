// methodology

From Field
to Genome

Our pipeline integrates classic microbiology with cutting-edge multi-omics approaches. From collecting water samples in volcanic craters to resolving population-level genomic variation — every step is designed to capture the full complexity of life in extreme geothermal environments.

01
Field Sampling
In-situ measurements, water and sediment collection across the full geothermal gradient. Physicochemical characterization on-site.
02
Isolation & Culture
Selective media, enrichment cultures, and strain isolation under acidic conditions. Growth characterization and preservation.
03
Metagenomics
Shotgun sequencing of community DNA. Assembly, binning, and MAG recovery to resolve community-level functional potential.
04
Genomics & Evolution
Whole genome sequencing, pan-genome analysis, population genomics. Detecting selection signatures and adaptive genes.
05
Global Databases
Cross-referencing with NCBI, JGI/IMG, SILVA, and public AMD metagenomes to contextualize local findings globally.
